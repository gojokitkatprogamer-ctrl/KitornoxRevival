ROBLOX [System Message]
The simple rules of ROBLOX, getting started!

Report Abuse
Hello there!

We want your time on ROBLOX to be fun and super awesome. To make that happen, everyone has to follow a few rules and try to be nice to each other.

Here are some basic rules of ROBLOX...

- No swear words
- No account sharing or trading
- No dating (for example, no asking for boyfriends or girlfriends)
- No asking real life info about each other - like no asking for phone numbers of email addresses

You can read the rest of the rules here:

http://www.roblox.com/help/builderman.aspx?sol=50140000000A3F9

Users who don't follow the rules will get a warning at first, but if they keep it up, we may ask them not to come to ROBLOX anymore. That way we can keep ROBLOX fun and safe!

If you have any other questions or need help, go check out Builderman's help pages and then send him a message. He'll get back to you!

-ROBLOX Staff
