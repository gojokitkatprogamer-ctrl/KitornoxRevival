  • ROBLOX allows kids to build amusement parks, movie theaters, skateboard parks, baseball fields, cruise ships and other sports venues.
  • ROBLOX allows children to invent toy cars, trucks, airplanes, pants, shirts, and hats.
  • ROBLOX allows children to create and sell snack foods, breakfast foods, energy drinks and fast food.
  • ROBLOX allows kids to create items like cell phones, video cameras, laptop computers and tv sets.
